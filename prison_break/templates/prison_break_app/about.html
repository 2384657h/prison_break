About

Prison break is a game created by Mark Harris, Andrew Parker Calum Macphee and Hector Jones. It was created for our 2nd year university project, for a web application course. There is only one objective in the game, to escape. You must do whatever it takes, and take advantage of all of your chosen characters skills to ensure a successful break out. Throughout the game you may find items, these are key components to your escape, and without them you will fail.
